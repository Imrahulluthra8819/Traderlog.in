Delivery Policy

Digital Delivery

TraderLog is a digital Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) product. No physical items will be shipped to you.

Instant Access

Upon successful payment or trial activation, you will receive an email immediately containing:

  • Your login credentials.
  • A link to access the dashboard.
  • Instructions on how to set up your journal.

Troubleshooting

If you do not receive your access email within 15 minutes of signing up, please:

  1. Check your Spam/Junk folder.
  2. Check the "Promotions" tab in Gmail.
  3. Contact our support team at support@traderlog.in.
