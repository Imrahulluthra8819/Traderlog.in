Refund Policy

Transparent and fair.

Last Updated: October 2023

1. 14-Day Free Trial

We strongly encourage all users to utilize the 14-Day Free Trial before purchasing a subscription. This allows you to fully test the features, analytics, and AI suggestions to ensure TraderLog meets your specific trading needs.

2. No Refunds on Subscriptions

Since we provide a fully functional free trial and our product is a digital service with instant access, we generally do not offer refunds once a subscription payment has been processed (Monthly, 6-Month, or Yearly).

3. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, your account will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle. You will not be charged again, but no pro-rated refund will be issued.

4. Exceptions

We may consider refunds in special circumstances, such as:

  • Technical Errors: If a system glitch causes a double charge.
  • Service Failure: If the platform is unavailable for more than 72 hours.

5. Contact Us

If you believe you have been charged in error, please contact us within 7 days at support@traderlog.in.
