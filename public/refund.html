Refund Policy

Fair and transparent.

Last Updated: October 2023

1. 14-Day Free Trial

We believe in our product. That's why we offer a 14-Day Free Trial for all new users. We strongly encourage you to use this period to fully test our AI analytics, journaling features, and reports to ensure TraderLog meets your needs before you pay.

2. No Refunds on Subscriptions

Since we provide a fully functional free trial and our product is a digital service with instant access, we generally do not offer refunds once a subscription payment has been processed (Monthly, 6-Month, or Yearly).

3. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, your account will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle. You will not be charged again, but no pro-rated refund will be issued for the remaining time.

4. Exceptions

We may consider refunds in special circumstances, such as:

  • Technical Errors: If a system glitch causes a double charge.
  • Service Failure: If the platform is unavailable for more than 72 hours due to our server issues.

5. Contact Us

If you believe you have been charged in error, please contact us within 7 days of the transaction at support@traderlog.in.
